It Is Easier To Let Go

It becomes clear to me
That it is tougher to hold on
That it is harder to keep someone around
And with every passing second
That I breath

It becomes clearer to me
That the person to hold unto is myself
Just like the sky
That never holds unto anything else

It becomes clear to me
That moving on is the way of nature
The sky let go of the rain no matter how refreshing;
let go of the moon no matter how bright;
let go of the stars no matter how beautiful;
let go of the sun no matter how shiny

It has become clearer to me
That no matter how long it takes
the rain, the sun, the moon, and the stars
they always come back to the sky and try again

And so it becomes even clearer to me
That just like the summer succeeds the winter
Good days will always succeed the bad days
And if we can just go back and try again
Our love will always come back to light
And we will hold unto us forever
Even though it is easier to let go
